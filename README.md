**Building tech for a better world.**

Open-source progressive civic-tech tools. Free for candidates and organizers who align with our values.

## Mission

The right wing pours billionaire money into political tech infrastructure. The left competes with people who believe in something bigger than themselves.

LeftyDevKit is the toolkit for that fight — built in public, one stream at a time.

Need a website, a tool, or tech help? **Free for progressive candidates and organizers.** Reach out — these tools are built on stream, for you.
